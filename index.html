About This Project

This website showcases original sonnets written by Aaron and Sanvir, two computer science enthusiasts who found inspiration in the intersection of technology and poetry.

Each sonnet explores themes of computation, algorithms, and the beauty of code through the classical form of a sonnet with 14 lines of iambic pentameter.

The design of this website is inspired by code editors with their syntax highlighting, line numbers, and minimalist interface, bringing together the aesthetic of programming and the elegance of poetry.
